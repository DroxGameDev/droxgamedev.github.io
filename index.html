About Me

Hi, my name is Alejandro, I'm 23 and I'm from Spain.

I'm a student in a videogames design and development degree at Universitat Jaume I in Castellón. I'm interested in several fields of game development, but above all I have a focus on programming, mechanics design and level design. My favorite videogame genre is the roguelike but a I've also preference for the metroidvania and action games. My biggest references in the industry are Dead Cells, Outer Wilds, Dishonored, TitanFall 2 and Katana Zero.

With each new project I try to make it different from the previous ones I have worked on in order to be constantly learning new skills and be able to improve my knowledge of the industry as much as possible.
