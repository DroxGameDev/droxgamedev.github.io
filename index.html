About Me

My name is Alejandro, I am 24 and I am from Spain.

I am a student in a videogames design and development degree at Universitat Jaume I in Castellón and working personal projects as an indie game developer. I am interested in several fields of game development but I have a focus on programming, mechanics design and level design. My favorite videogame genre is the metroidvania but a I have also a preference for roguelikes and action games. My biggest references in the industry are Hollow Knight, Dead Cells, Outer Wilds, Dishonored, TitanFall 2 and Katana Zero.

With each new project I try to make it different from the previous ones I have worked on in order to be constantly learning new skills and be able to improve my knowledge of the industry as much as possible.
